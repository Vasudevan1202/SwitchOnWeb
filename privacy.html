Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to handling your information carefully and responsibly.

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve SwitchOn and the overall user experience

3. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

4. Third-Party Services

SwitchOn may use third-party services such as Firebase for storage and analytics tools such as Google Analytics in the future, in accordance with their respective privacy practices.

Minimal cookies may be used to improve website functionality and support key user experience features.

6. User Rights

Users may request correction or deletion of their information by contacting the founder at founder@switchon.qzz.io.

The website is intended for general audiences. Personal information from minors is handled responsibly and only where necessary for the stated purpose.

8. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or the law. Continued use of the website after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact founder@switchon.qzz.io.
