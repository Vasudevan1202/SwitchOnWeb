Meet the founder behind SwitchOn.

SwitchOn is currently a solo-founded startup, driven by one clear mission: create simple, practical electronics that solve everyday problems.

Vasudevan

Founder & Product Lead

Vasudevan is the founder of SwitchOn, a student-led startup focused on solving everyday problems with practical electronics. His mission is to make charging reliability feel effortless, affordable, and built for real life.

Founder story

After seeing how often people forget a simple wall switch, he began turning the idea into a product that helps users save time and avoid frustration.
